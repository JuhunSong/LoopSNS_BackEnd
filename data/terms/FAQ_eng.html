Frequently asked questions



Members


● How do I sign up?


To sign up, follow these steps.

⇒

1. Download the 'Loop' application from the Google Play store.

2. Sign in with your Google or Kakao account.

3. You will be logged in Once you have verified your Google or Kakao account.



● How do I log out?


To log out, follow these steps.

⇒

1. From the 'Home' screen, go to 'Profile' on the left-hand side.

2. Click Settings in the top right corner.

3. Log out by clicking the 'Log Out' button in the 'Manage Account' section.



● How do I log in with a different ID?


To log in with a different username, follow the steps below.

⇒

1. From the 'Home' screen, go to 'Profile' on the left-hand side.

2. Click Settings in the top right corner.

3. Click the 'Sign out' button in the 'Manage account' section.

4. Log out and select your new account on the Start screen to sign in.



● How do I unsubscribe?


To cancel your membership, follow the steps below.

⇒

1. From the 'Home' screen, go to 'Profile' on the left-hand side.

2. Click Settings in the top right corner.

3. Click the 'Delete account' button in the 'Manage Account' section.

4. To delete your account, type in 'Delete' and click the 'Delete' button.



● Can minors sign up?


You can create an account if you have a Google or Kakao account.



● How can I set the language to English or Korean?


To set the language, follow these steps.

⇒

1. From the 'Home' screen, go to 'Profile' on the left-hand side.

2. Click Settings in the top right corner.

3. Select your preferred language. Click the 'Set language' button.

4. Click 'Yes' to restart to complete the language settings.



● How can I change my profile picture and name?


To edit your profile, follow these steps.

⇒

1. From the 'Home' screen, go to 'Profile' on the left-hand side.

2. Click Settings in the top right corner.

3. Click the 'Edit Personal Information' button in the 'Privacy Settings' section.

4. Edit your name and profile picture, then click the Save button.



● I want to delete a post that I created.


To delete a post you've created, follow these steps.

⇒

1. Click on the post you want to delete to go to the 'Post details' screen.

2. Click the button in the top right corner.

3. Click the 'Delete post' button in the menu that appears to delete it.

4. You can only delete your posts.



● I want to report a potentially problematic post.


To report a post, follow the steps below.

⇒

1. Click on the post you'd like to report to go to the 'Post details' screen.

2. Click the button in the top right corner.

3. Click the 'Report' button at the bottom of the menu that appears to make a report.

4. Reported posts will be reviewed and either sanctioned or removed.
